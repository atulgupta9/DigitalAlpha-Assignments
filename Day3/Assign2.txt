There are two ways to summarize them for EDA.
1.	Non-Graphical Summaries(Numerical)
2.	Graphical Summaries

1.	Non-Graphical Summaries(Numerical)
  a.	Mean
  b.	Median
  c.	Mode
  d.	Variance and Standard Deviation (Measures the spread of distribution)
  
2.	Graphical Summaries
  a.	Pareto Chart -> For categorical data
  b.	Histogram -> For batch of numerical data
  c.	Boxplots -> For numerical data
  d.	Time Series Plot -> For data collected over time
  e.	Scatterplot
